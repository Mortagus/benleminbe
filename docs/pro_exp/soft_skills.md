# Soft Skills

## Overview

This document lists the professional and interpersonal skills developed throughout the career of **Benjamin Lemin**.

These soft skills have been built through more than 15 years of experience working on web platforms across multiple industries, within consulting environments, product teams and collaborative development teams.

The skills listed below complement technical expertise and reflect the ability to work effectively within real-world software development environments.

## Understanding Complex Systems

Strong ability to analyze and understand existing software systems, including large or legacy codebases.

Typical situations include:

- understanding interactions between multiple services

This skill has been developed through years of working on evolving production platforms where full rewrites are rarely possible.

## Problem Solving

Ability to investigate technical issues and identify practical solutions.

This includes:

- debugging production problems

- balancing technical quality with project constraints
